# SEBON Compliance Notes (Educational Platform)

Last updated: March 5, 2026

## Purpose
This document tracks legal and compliance guardrails for ArthaPath Nepal as an education-first investment planning platform.

## Compliance Positioning
- The platform is for financial education and planning support.
- The platform does not provide personalized investment advice.
- The platform does not execute trades or place securities orders.
- All return ranges and projections are estimates and assumptions-based.

## Implemented Safeguards
- Global disclaimer banner is shown in the application shell.
- Dedicated Disclaimer, Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy pages are available.
- Risk acknowledgment modal is required before first high-risk simulator selection.
- Product copy avoids guaranteed-return or risk-free claims in user-facing features.
- Analytics and error monitoring disclosures are included in privacy language.

## Operational Controls
- Audit logging is enabled for investment category changes.
- Data verification script validates seeded category consistency.
- Access control remains role-based for admin management routes.
